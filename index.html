  • Efficient Probabilistic Occluded-object Pose Estimation

    Master Thesis

    • We tried to estimate the 6DoF poses of multiple objects from a single RGB image where objects are partially occluded.
    • We considered the uncertainty of the predicted locations and formulate the probabilistic locations in our proposed loss.
    • We proposed a 6D pose estimation network which predicts the 2D locations together with localization variance, and then solve the 6DoF poses by PnP algorithm.
    • We integrated the most confident predictions(lower localization variance) and further improve the performance.
    • Our method outperformed the state-of-the-arts on Occlusion LINEMOD dataset (a challenging benchmark in which the objects are frequently occluded).
